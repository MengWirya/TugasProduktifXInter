Welcome to the library!

Welcome dear guest, to the City biggest library, Library of Ruina!
Here, we provide countless books with each of them capable providing all the wisdom, wealth, honor, and power you seek.
However, one should not disturb the peace that we offered. Our librarian here is not to be messed up.

While you may read the books directly, such behavior is not suggested. Any 'scene' happening from that is not our responsibilities.
Please feel free to contribute by asking our assistant here, all contribution will be remember deeply forever...
We hope you enjoyed your experienced reading here, if possible we wish you share our 'Invitation' to peoples around the City.

- Our Librarians Poems -


Prayer For A
Loving Sorrow


- By. Roland

I have nothing but my sorrow… And I want nothing more. It has been, it still is, faithful to me.
should I begrudge it, since during the hours when my soul crushed the depths of my heart, it was seated there beside me?
sorrow, I have ended, you see, by respecting you, because I am certain you will never leave me.
Ah, I realize it! Your beauty lies in the force of your being. You are like those who never left the sad fireside corner of my poor black heart.
O my sorrow, you are better than a well-beloved: because I know that on the day of my final agony, you will be there, lying in my sheets. O sorrow. So that you might once again attempt to enter my heart.
